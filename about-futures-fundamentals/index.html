About Futures Fundamentals

Futures Fundamentals: A clear look at the world of derivatives

Think this is all over your head? Think again. Futures Fundamentals is CME Group's one-stop educational resource for simplifying the world of derivatives for learners of all levels.

We believe that futures help the world advance. Every second of every day, we enable people, institutions, governments and organizations around the world to manage the risk of doing business so they can move confidently forward – furthering their business goals and ultimately advancing the global economy.

Obviously, we know our business well – but we also recognize that many others don’t. Futures Fundamentals was created to help educate the general public about the role futures play in the global economy, and the impact they have on everyday life.

About CME Group

As the world's leading and most diverse derivatives marketplace, CME Group is where the world comes to manage risk. Comprised of four exchanges – CME, CBOT, NYMEX and COMEX – we offer the widest range of global benchmark products across all major industries, including futures and options based on agricultural commodities, metals, energy, interest rates, equity indexes, foreign exchange, weather and real estate. We help businesses everywhere mitigate the myriad of risks they face in today's uncertain global economy.
