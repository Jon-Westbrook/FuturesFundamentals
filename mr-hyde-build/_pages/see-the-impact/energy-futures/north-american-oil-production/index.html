North American Oil Production: The Rapid Move to Energy Self-Sufficiency

North America has long relied on foreign oil markets to meet its energy needs. But due to recent advances in extraction, domestic production of crude oil is at an all-time high. In fact, it’s estimated that North America will achieve energy self-sufficiency by 2020 – a milestone that will have a huge impact, not just on the global oil markets, but also on the United States’ economy and infrastructure.
